Product/Service Page(s):
Purpose: To give a detailed breakdown of the product or service, its features, and benefits.

Sales Copy: Use informative content, embed calls to action, use search engine optimisation techniques, and promote the product/service in a natural manner.
Visuals: High-quality images, graphics, and videos to go with the written content.

Features: Collect user feedback, use star ratings, and include customer images and videos wherever possible.
Sales Copy: Focus on how these testimonials highlight the value, quality, and efficacy of the offering.
Visuals: Use images, quotes, and videos of happy users.

Purpose: To provide a way for customers to reach you for support, questions, or further information.
Features: A contact form, email address, phone number, and a map showing any relevant business addresses.
Sales Copy: Provide helpful ways for the user to contact you, provide a warm message, and ensure quick turnaround on messages to instill trust and provide excellent customer care.
Visuals: A friendly image or design that encourages visitors to reach out.

Purpose: Address common questions, eliminate barriers to purchase, and build confidence.
Features: Organized questions and clear answers regarding the product or service. Questions should be based on the most common pain points or inquiries.
Sales Copy: The answers should be informative and reassuring, overcoming doubts and reassuring the user that they are making the right decision.
Visuals: Use a clean and organized layout to ensure the FAQ is easy to use.

Purpose: If you intend to collaborate with other businesses or affiliates, this page can show collaborations and improve the overall credibility of your website.
Features: Display partner logos, link to partners, and add a short description.
Sales Copy: Explain how partnerships will benefit customers and showcase the value of the collaborations.
Visuals: High-resolution logos, images, and potentially a short video about the partners.
Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions:
Purpose: To ensure legal compliance and protect user information.
Features: Standard legal text, clearly visible on each page.
Sales Copy: It is recommended to have legal advisors draw up these sections and review them to ensure legal compliance.
